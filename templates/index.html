Fireworks in Minneapolis

I am asking the City of Minneapolis for the fireworks permits they have issued and posting about them online. You can follow @FireworksInMpls on Twitter for updates. I typically tweet once around noon the day of the fireworks, and once 15-30 minutes before the fireworks.

You can add the firework permits to your calendar program. Add as a web calendar or view the iCalendar file directly.

I have also posted the raw spreadsheets I received from the City of Minneapolis, if you would like more details.
